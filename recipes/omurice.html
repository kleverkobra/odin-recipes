Omurice

Description

Omurice or omu-rice (オムライス, Omu-raisu) is a Japanese dish consisting of an omelette made with fried rice and thin, fried scrambled eggs, usually topped with ketchup. It is a popular dish also commonly cooked at home. The base of any omurice recipe starts with the fried rice, made with soft scrambled eggs and rice that's flavored with ketchup and a splash of soy sauce. It's savory and soft in texture; a comfort food for both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Cooked rice
  • 1 Tbsp Ketchup
  • Chicken Thigh
  • Half Onion
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Make the Rice
  2. Stir Onion with ketchup and pepper in butter
  3. Add Rice to the pan
  4. Stir until rice is equally red
  5. Make the omlette
  6. Add the omlette on the rice
